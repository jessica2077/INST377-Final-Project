About To&FROM

Closing communication gaps to foster inclusivity in so many different places.

What Problem are we addressing?

In classrooms, workplaces, healthcare settings, and customer service environments, individuals whose primary language differs from the dominant language face a persistent barrier when trying to comprehend vital written or spoken information. English as a Second Language (ESL) students, immigrants, and multilingual communities face systemic disadvantages when accessing materials needed for education, professional settings, or public services that fail to support their native tongue.

While commercial services exist, they lack accessible, real-time integration into everyday digital workflow spaces, presenting inconveniences that isolate non-native speakers.

Our Mission & Goal

The goal of To&FROM is to address and resolve these accessibility issues by delivering a fast, light, and intuitive web-based translation interface.

Key Stakeholders

Our platform is engineered to directly empower and serve our communities:

  • ESL Students & Immigrants: Gaining access to instructional materials and learning tools.
  • Universities & The General Public Institutions: Creating inclusive settings for highly diverse populations.
  • Companies & Businesses: Minimizing operational friction and ensuring safety across multilingual corporate environments.
  • Customer Service Professionals: Reducing misunderstandings and elevating client experiences.
